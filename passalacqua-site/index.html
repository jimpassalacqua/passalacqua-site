Common questions

How do you typically work with clients?

Every engagement starts with a conversation about your challenge, your goals, and what success looks like. From there I scope a project, we agree on the approach, and I get to work. I stay close throughout and you're never out of the loop.

Do you work with small businesses or just larger brands?

Both. My background includes major entertainment and consumer brands, but some of my most satisfying work has been helping smaller businesses figure out who they are and what to say. Good creative thinking doesn't require a big budget.

What does a project cost?

Every project is different so I don't publish rates, but I work on a project basis, not hourly. You get a clear scope and a flat fee before anything starts. No surprises.

How long does a typical engagement take?

Depends on the scope. A creative strategy or brand sprint can happen in a week or two. A fuller campaign development engagement is typically four to eight weeks. I'll always tell you upfront what to expect.

What makes you different from an agency?

You get me, not a team of junior staff managed by someone you rarely see. I'm the one thinking through your challenge, developing the ideas, and delivering the work. No layers, no handoffs, no surprises.

Can you work with my existing creative team?

Absolutely. In fact that's often where I add the most value. I can work alongside your internal team or agency, bringing senior creative direction and outside perspective without disrupting what's already working.
