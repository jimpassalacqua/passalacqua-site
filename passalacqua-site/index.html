What I do

01

Creative Strategy

Brand · Campaign · Direction

Sometimes the work just isn't landing and nobody can quite put their finger on why. The messaging feels off. The creative has drifted. The campaign looks fine but doesn't feel right. I find the thread and pull everything back into focus.

Whether you're launching something new, rethinking something existing, or just stuck, I bring an outside perspective and the experience to know what needs to change and what doesn't.

  • Brand positioning and messaging
  • Campaign concepting and direction
  • Creative audits and honest feedback
  • Storytelling for brands without a story yet
02

Fractional Creative Direction

Leadership · Oversight · Partnership

You need senior creative leadership but not a full-time hire. I work with your team on a project basis, raising the bar on the work and helping your people do their best.

I work remotely and stay close through the creative process, showing up in person for kickoffs and key milestones when it matters most. You get the thinking of someone who has directed creative at LucasArts and Francis Ford Coppola Winery without the overhead of a full-time CD.

  • Creative oversight and quality control
  • Team feedback and creative development
  • Remote-first with onsite kickoffs and milestones
  • Project-based engagements, no long-term contracts required
03

Ideation and Concepting

Ideas · Brainstorm · Possibilities

Ideas come naturally to me. Always have. Give me a product, a problem, or a blank page and I will fill it. This is where I'm most in my element and where clients tend to get the most out of working with me.

Whether you need a fresh campaign concept, a new way to talk about your brand, or someone to push your thinking in a direction you haven't considered, this is the work I was built for.

  • Campaign and concept development
  • New product and brand launches
  • Creative brainstorms and working sessions
  • Turning a product with no story into one worth telling

How I work

All engagements are project-based, scoped around what you actually need. I work remotely and stay close throughout the process, showing up in person for the moments that benefit most from being together. No hourly billing, no rigid retainers. Just focused work with a clear goal and a commitment to getting it right.
